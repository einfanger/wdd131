Overview

Purpose

My purpose of this website is to give all travellers a quick, fun, and confidence-boosting way to explore three of the world's most beautiful cities, Tokyo, Paris, and New York City. Instead of scrolling through Yelp or outdated activies, users get a curated, modern list of the best restaraunts and activites that never feel out of style, and always hit the beat.

Audience

I want my audince to be people who are exctied about travelling, and want to find only the best things to do in the cities they want to go to, without the hassle of planning. I am hoping specifically for famillies, young couples who want to take a trip, and older couples who are ready to travel.

Dynamic elements

My site will use a lot of dynamic JS. I am hoping to add smooth animations, as I want my website to be sleek, like the PS5, and users will be able to click on the city they want to visit, and be guided instantly to curated lists. It will also handle themed city color changes, smooth transitions, and possibly animations such as slideshows for images.

Content

Home page

The home page will show three large clickable city cards: Tokyo, Paris, and New York City. Each card will include an image and a short description of the city. It will also show what is seen in the wireframe.

Tokyo Page

Sukiyabashi Jiro – A world-renowned sushi restaurant famed for its meticulous omakase experience. Every piece is crafted with precision, offering an unforgettable taste of traditional Edo-style sushi.
Ichiran Ramen – Specializes in tonkotsu ramen, with a focus on customizable flavor and individual dining booths that provide a unique, immersive experience.
Ginza Kyubey – Offers high-quality sushi in Tokyo’s luxurious Ginza district. Known for its attentive service and expertly prepared seasonal fish.
Tempura Kondo – A Michelin-starred destination for tempura lovers, with a light, crisp batter that elevates fresh, seasonal ingredients.
Narisawa – Celebrated for innovative Japanese cuisine that blends traditional flavors with modern culinary artistry, earning international acclaim.
Shibuya Crossing – One of the world’s busiest pedestrian intersections, offering a vibrant urban spectacle and quintessential Tokyo experience.
Senso-ji Temple – Tokyo’s oldest temple, surrounded by lively markets and historical architecture, providing a deep dive into Japanese culture.
Tokyo Skytree – The tallest tower in Japan, offering panoramic views across the sprawling cityscape and beyond.
TeamLab Borderless – A cutting-edge digital art museum that immerses visitors in interactive, ever-changing light and sound installations.
Meiji Shrine – A peaceful Shinto shrine nestled within a lush forest in central Tokyo, providing a serene retreat from the city bustle.

Paris Page

Le Meurice – A Michelin-starred restaurant blending exquisite French cuisine with luxurious interior design, located in the heart of Paris.
L’Ambroisie – Renowned for classic French haute cuisine, offering a refined dining experience in the historic Place des Vosges.
Septime – Combines modern culinary innovation with fresh, seasonal ingredients in a relaxed yet elegant setting.
Pierre Hermé – Famous worldwide for intricate pastries and delicate macarons, blending creativity and precision in every bite.
Bistrot Paul Bert – A quintessential Parisian bistro serving traditional French dishes with authentic flavors and a warm, inviting atmosphere.
Eiffel Tower – The emblematic Parisian landmark, offering breathtaking city views from its observation decks. A must-visit for first-time travelers.
Louvre Museum – Houses thousands of masterpieces, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo, making it a central hub for art and history enthusiasts.
Notre-Dame Cathedral – Iconic Gothic cathedral with intricate architecture, stained glass windows, and centuries of cultural significance.
Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur – A charming, historic district with cobblestone streets, art studios, and panoramic views from the hilltop basilica.
Seine River Cruise – Offers a scenic perspective of Paris landmarks, especially magical at night when bridges and buildings are illuminated.

New York City Page

Le Bernardin – Michelin-starred seafood restaurant celebrated for its precise technique, fresh ingredients, and sophisticated French-inspired dishes.
Katz’s Delicatessen – Historic deli serving legendary pastrami sandwiches, preserving a slice of classic New York culinary tradition.
Peter Luger Steak House – Old-school steakhouse offering expertly aged cuts in a no-frills, iconic Manhattan setting.
Momofuku Noodle Bar – Trendy and innovative, blending Asian-inspired flavors with casual dining for a modern, flavorful experience.
Russ & Daughters – Famous for smoked fish, bagels, and traditional Jewish delicacies, offering authentic flavors that define New York’s culinary history.
Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island – Historical symbols of freedom and immigration, offering insight into America’s past with breathtaking harbor views.
Central Park – Expansive urban park featuring gardens, lakes, walking paths, and iconic attractions, perfect for outdoor exploration.
Times Square – The vibrant heart of Manhattan, renowned for dazzling lights, entertainment, and the city’s iconic energy.
Empire State Building – Offers panoramic views from its observation decks, showcasing Manhattan’s skyline day and night.
Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met) – One of the world’s largest and most prestigious art museums, featuring art spanning centuries and continents.
